Our Story

Authentic Street Food,
Made Fresh Daily

Welcome to Chatoras Food — where the vibrant flavours of Indian street food are elevated with care, craft, and uncompromising quality.

From our signature crispy puffs to tangy pani puri and hearty vada pav, every dish honours authentic recipes and the finest ingredients. Born from a passion for असली स्वाद, we bring the soul of India's food streets to Orpington.
